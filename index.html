yet to take, the roads we’ll travel together.

been a step closer to a future where our dreams
intertwine.

adventures, where every day brings us closer.

a time,

love, and endless possibilities.

our own tale,

realized.

to cherish every moment, every chapter, as we
write our forever together.
Happy Birthday, my dear, and here’s to the many
